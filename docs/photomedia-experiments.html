Experiments generator

Try something new with your composite

This works best when the composition is done, and you are trying different things to change the look or feeling of the image. The aim is to do this non-destructively; work with adjustment layers, or work on a stamped layer (⇧⌥⌘E). Generate one experiment at a time. You need to document two experiments for your AT3d Report. Even if an experiment does not work out, it is still valuable documentation that shows your process.
Remember: save each experiment as a screenshot or JPEG. Write three sentences: what you tried, what you hoped to achieve, and why it did or did not work.
