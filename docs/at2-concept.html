Use this once per concept. Each generate gives you a starting direction — you decide how far to take it. Aim for three concepts that are distinct from each other but work together as a range. One package presented well is enough to represent the full collection.
Your process for each concept:
Step 1

Do some light visual research into the concept direction — not to find answers, but to build a visual vocabulary. Go deeper into what the mood board is pointing at.

Step 2

Sketch 12 thumbnails — quick visualisations of what a package in this style could look like. The generator gives you a direction, not a prescription. Use the sketching prompt if you need a way in.

Step 3

Create a rough showing one package plus the design system elements: pattern, colour swatches, type treatment. This is what goes in your presentation.
