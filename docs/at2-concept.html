Design considerations

T-shirt placement: One concept becomes a placement print. Consider which design will maintain impact at chest or sleeve scale.

System thinking: Ensure your design approach works cohesively across five packages and the t-shirt while allowing for variety within the range.

Time constraints: Choose techniques you can execute well within the timeframe. A confident marker sketch often beats a rushed digital illustration.

Reproduction quality: Remember this will be scanned or photographed for client presentation. Ensure sufficient contrast and clarity.

Concept clarity: The technique should support, not overshadow, your design thinking.
