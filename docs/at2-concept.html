Design considerations

T-shirt placement: One concept becomes a placement print. Consider which design will maintain impact at chest or sleeve scale.

Design thinking: Ensure your design approach works cohesively across five packages + t-shirt while allowing for variety within the range.

Time constraints: Choose techniques you can execute well within the timeframe. A confident marker sketch often beats a rushed digital illustration.

Presentation quality: Remember this will be scanned or photographed for presentation, and will likely be re-used in your folio. Ensure sufficient contrast and clarity, and your photos are well-lit.

Concept clarity: The technique you pick should match the design ideas. For example, some ideas are better represented with Copic markers, others might look better with cut-out craft paper.
